**Dr. Zhichen Lai** is a Research Associate Professor at the [College of Computer and Data Science](https://ccds.fzu.edu.cn/), [Fuzhou University](https://en.fzu.edu.cn/), China. He is also affiliated with the [Fujian Provincial Key Laboratory of Network Computing and Intelligent Information Processing](https://ncip.fzu.edu.cn/) and was selected for the *China's Ministry-Level Overseas Talent Introduction Program* in 2025.

Previously, he was a Postdoctoral Fellow working with [Prof. Hua Lu](https://homes.cs.aau.dk/~luhua/). He earned his Ph.D. in Computer Science from [Aalborg University](https://www.en.aau.dk/) in 2025, advised by [Prof. Christian S. Jensen](https://csj.cs.aau.dk/) (ACM/IEEE Fellow, MAE), [Prof. Dalin Zhang](https://dalinzhang.github.io/), and [Prof. Huan Li](https://longaspire.github.io/). During his doctoral studies, he served as a Junior Visiting Research Fellow at [UNSW Sydney](https://www.unsw.edu.au/). Earlier, he received his M.S. in Computer Science from [Sichuan University](https://en.scu.edu.cn/) in 2021 under the supervision of [Prof. Jiancheng Lv](https://cs.scu.edu.cn/info/1288/13627.htm), and his B.S. in Computer Science from [UESTC](https://en.uestc.edu.cn/) in 2018.

**His research interests include:**
- **Spatio-Temporal Edge Intelligence**
- **Lightweight Deep Learning**
- **IoT Data Mining**
- **AI-Driven Digital Orthodontics** *(with Prof. Linyu Xu's Group, Department of Orthodontics, FJMU)*
